About Sparx

Sparx is the flagship event of Engineers Week, bringing together brilliant minds and innovative ideas. Organized by the EMAS Committee, this event showcases the best of engineering talent through a diverse range of technical and non-technical competitions.

Whether you're passionate about coding, Fitness, design, or gaming to showcase your non-technical skills, Sparx offers something for everyone. Join us for a week of learning, competition, and networking.
